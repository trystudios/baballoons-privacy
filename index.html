Privacy Policy

App: Shoot & Pop BaBalloons  |  Package: com.trystudios.baballoons  |  Effective: April 8, 2026

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your highest unlocked game level
  • Total number of rounds played
  • Display preferences (archer visibility and position)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Advertising

This app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Approximate location (derived from IP)
  • Usage data related to ad interactions

Google's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

To opt out of personalized advertising, go to your device's Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Permissions Used

PermissionReason
INTERNETRequired to load and display ads via Google AdMob
VIBRATEHaptic feedback during gameplay (arrow release, balloon pop)

No camera, microphone, location, contacts, or storage permissions are used.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can address it.

Data Security

All game progress is stored locally using Android's SharedPreferences. No data is transmitted to external servers by this app.
